PostgreSQL Join
Algorithm Research

My primary research contribution is modifying PostgreSQL's nested loop join executor (src/backend/executor/nodeNestloop.c) to implement and benchmark adaptive approximation strategies against the simple baseline.

Traditional joins require complete table scans before returning results. I'm implementing and comparing online/adaptive strategies that return statistically confident approximate results mid-execution — enabling early termination on large datasets like TPC-H 10GB.

Two statistical estimators — Des Raj and Horvitz-Thompson — are evaluated across three LV configurations, with accuracy plotted as a function of output percentage.
